What is the difference between gross and take home

Gross is what your revenue is BEFORE taxes are taken out.

What other revenue can count?

Any revenue such as a second job - please count 50% of the gross takehome. Now if this second revenue is from a partner we'll count 100% of that revenue.. but since it's a second revenue stream for one person the House Gods decide it 50%. We cannot anger them. If there is any foreign income good luck with having that count. It's income but we choose not to count it just to make life interesting.

Major Expenses / Debt - what should this count as?

Cars, cats, dogs, children expenses are x200 because anger the House Gods choose so. Plus legit question, how are you expected to afford a kid AND a house. Another hurdle just to make living in Dublin interesting.

According to your calculator I can afford €285k with 20% down. Is this right?

Welcome to reality. Would you like a drink? We've reached a place where earning a decent salary for the area still makes it utterly useless in Dublin. Until you can put down over 50% is when you shall be able to own a little more than a shoebox. Well done. You have won the house wars.
